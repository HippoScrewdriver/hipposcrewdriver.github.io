About Me

Hello, I'm Vesper.

I believe every wedding holds a universe of small, irreplaceable moments — the nervous laugh before the vows, the tear your mother tries to hide, the way light falls across the dance floor just after sunset. My job is simply to be there, quietly, and let those moments breathe.

我相信每一場婚禮，都藏著無數珍貴的瞬間——宣誓前那一聲緊張的笑，媽媽悄悄拭去的眼淚，夕陽後光線灑落舞台的模樣。我的工作，是安靜地在場，讓這些瞬間自然呼吸。
