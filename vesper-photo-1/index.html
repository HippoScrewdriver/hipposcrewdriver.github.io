关于我

Hello，我是 Vesper

我相信，婚礼中最美的瞬间，往往不是刻意摆拍的，而是那些眼神交汇、轻声低语、被人群包围却只看着彼此的片刻。

带着一台相机，我静静地走进你们的故事——不打扰，不制造，只是在对的瞬间，按下快门。每一张照片，都是你们真实情感的印记。

I believe the most beautiful wedding moments are never posed — they are the glances, the whispers, and the quiet seconds when two people see only each other in a crowd.

With my camera I step gently into your story — never interrupting, never staging, just waiting for the right heartbeat to press the shutter. Every photograph is an honest imprint of your love.
